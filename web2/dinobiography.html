Backstory of Dino-kun

I've started to draw when I was very young. Drawing has been one of my favorite things to do in life other than playing games, watching Anime and thinking about food.

Family

I was born on April 20 2000 in Baltimore, Maryland. Like my siblings before and after me, I was given a multiple nicknames; one of them being Dino.

Family

I am the third youngest child in a family of six and also the youngest male. I have a mother, a father, two older brothers and a little sister.

OCs to Series

As a kid, I would create weird and interesting comics/illustrations. For example, I drew Spongebob as a Pokemon Trainer and as Mario. I even made a Sonic OC; I named him Malcolm the Hedgehog and if your a die hard Sonic fan, you might assume that he's a mix between Sonic, Shadow and Scrouge. When I was a preteen, I thought about making Malcolm into a human...like in an Anime like style. This idea was interesting, little did I know that it would change my life...or rather what I want to do in life. During High School, I started to create my characters in an Anime style of my liking, which inspired me to create my own Anime/Manga series.
