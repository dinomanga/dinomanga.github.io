Inspiration

Various manga and anime like Dragon Ball, One Piece, Naruto, My Hero Academia and many more have inspired me to create my own shounen series. Over more than a decade, my drawing style has improved greatly from stick figures to anime style drawing. When it comes to my drawing style, both Dragon Ball and My Hero Academia provides a major influence when comes to creating facial expressions and clothes.
