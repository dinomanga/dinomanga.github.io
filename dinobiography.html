Bio

My name is Faraji Qiydaar, but I also go by Dino-kun. I've started to draw when I was in kindergarden and never stop. Drawing has been one of my favorite things to do in life other than playing games, watching Anime and thinking about food.

Birth

I was born on April 20, 2000 in Baltimore, Maryland. As a kid, I was given multiple nicknames; one of them being Dino.

Career

My goal in life is to become a graphic artist. I am currently working on my own shounen manga series and hope to create a franchise in the future.

Experiences

As a kid, I would create weird and interesting comics/illustrations. Long story short, I made an oc character and decided to make him into a human. This idea was interesting, little did I know that it would change my life...or rather what I want to do in life. During High School, I started to create my characters in an Anime style of my liking, which inspired me to create my own Anime/Manga series.

During High School, my drawing skills have developed greatly to the point where I help paint murals within Baltimore. I've even sold some of my art and was offered to sell a canvas as I focus on my schoolwork and early college classes.
